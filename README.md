I'm Angel and I am an Electrical Engineer with a Ph.D. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering exploring the development of AI-assisted code to solve problems in power distribution engineering. I am passionate about mathematical optimization, more electric aircraft, and classical music as well. 

Currently working on power distribution algorithms for the analysis of distribution networks: [check it here](https://github.com/angelrecalde2024/Power-Distribution-Design-and-Management)

---

I have taught Power Distribution and Power Transmission courses for Master's in Electrical Engineering at ESPOL, Ecuador. 

Since proprietary professional software is difficult to obtain in an academic environment due to highly restrictive prices, I bet for education based on open-source platforms like OpenDSS. For the rest of the endeavours I prefer to construct micro-analysis tools (Python notebooks) to teach concepts using case-analysis with real life data. 

Projects I've worked on include: 

* [Power System Planning and Transmission Design](https://github.com/angelrecalde2024/Power-System-Planning-and-Transmission-Design-2026): An open source project that explores several analysis in transmission networks including generation expansion, transmission expansion, and also load forecast.

I will continue to elaborate in several repoitories as I discover how AI can assist in creating tools that enhace learning experiences.
